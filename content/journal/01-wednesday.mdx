Some things start with a plan. This did not.

I have been keeping notes for a while — observations about AI tools, things that surprised me, things that did not work the way I expected. They lived in a Notion doc that I never shared with anyone. At some point I started wondering whether they were worth making public.

The honest answer is that I do not know. But I am doing it anyway.

The working title for this section is *Out of Scope*. The name comes from a phrase I use in product work to describe features or requirements that exist outside the defined boundaries of a project. They are real, they matter, they just do not belong in the current sprint.

Most of what I think about falls into that category. It is not a pitch. It is not a deliverable. It is just what I notice.

That felt like a reasonable place to start.
